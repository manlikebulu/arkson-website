— Before you ask

Common questions

What's the minimum order size you'll quote?

We quote from single prototype parts up through production runs of tens of thousands. Prototype and low-volume work is priced differently from production tooling — ask for both if you're unsure which stage you're at.

Can you work from a sketch instead of a finished CAD file?

Yes. A large share of our design work starts from a napkin sketch or a rough requirement. We'll take it through concept, modeling, and DFM review before anything is quoted for machining.

What materials do you machine?

Aluminum, steel and stainless alloys, titanium, brass, and common engineering plastics (Delrin, PEEK, nylon, UHMW). If your material isn't listed, ask — we source most alloys on request.

Do you provide inspection documentation?

Every production batch ships with a CMM dimensional report and material certification. First-article inspection reports are available for any new part on request.

How long does a typical quote take?

Most quotes return within 2 business days once we have drawings or a clear spec. Complex assemblies or new materials may take longer — we'll tell you upfront if that's the case.
